Spaceship Idle

  • Joey Vigil
  • Mobile · Android
  • Jul 17, 2026

Command a lone survey ship pushing into uncharted space. Distance from home is your progression axis. The voyage is a stream of varied encounters — hostiles, anomalies, derelicts, first contacts, hazards — each resolved by a different ship system. Nothing can be skipped: deep space gets harder until the only sane move is to return home, publish your findings, and launch a better ship. Optimistic, TNG-style exploration in your pocket.

The core loop

  • Your ship travels outward and lightyears tick up; encounters spawn along the path.
  • Every encounter must be resolved by its matching system — weak stats just mean slow, never a fail.
  • Encounters produce Data, codex entries and rare Exotic Matter.
  • Spend Data on ship systems to resolve faster, travel faster, and automate more.
  • Difficulty outpaces your yields, so eventually the frontier grinds you to a halt.

Return home to go further

When deep space finally pushes back, return home to convert the voyage into Renown, buy permanent upgrades, and launch a better ship that pushes farther before it grinds down. The wall isn't a menu you're nagged about — you watch your ship take longer and longer per encounter and feel the frontier resisting.

Five encounters, five systems

  • Hostiles — met with Weapons. Pirates and aggressive fauna.
  • Anomalies — met with Sensors. Nebulae and spatial phenomena; the richest Data.
  • Derelicts — met with the Salvage Rig. Wrecks and dead stations.
  • First Contact — met with the Comm Array. New lifeforms fill your codex.
  • Hazards — met with Deflectors. Clear one for a charted shortcut and a speed boost.

An idle game you can dip into for seconds or watch for hours. In development — coming to Android.
