Thirty One Blitz

  • Joey Vigil
  • Nov 16, 2023

A game I learned from some Austrians. Game will include a 36 card deck as well as 18 coins in a small box.

Objective

Each player starts with 3 coins. Players who lose all their coins are eliminated. The last player with coins wins.

Setup

Players take turns being the dealer.

  1. The dealer shuffles the deck.
  2. The dealer deals 3 cards to every other player.
  3. The dealer looks at the top 3 cards and chooses whether they will be:
    • their starting hand, or
    • the 3 face-up center cards.
  4. The next 3 cards become whichever was not chosen, so that both the dealer and the center have 3 cards.

The player clockwise from the dealer goes first. Play continues clockwise.

On Your Turn

Choose one action:

  • Trade One Card: Swap 1 card from your hand with 1 center card.
  • Trade Three Cards: Exchange your entire hand with the center cards.
  • Skip: Make no trade.
  • Knock: End the round. All other players take one final turn, then all hands are revealed.

Scoring

Card values:

  • 6–10 = Face value
  • U (Unter), O (Ober), K (King) = 10
  • A = 11

Your score is the highest possible total from any one suit in your hand.

Example: U♥, K♥, A♠ = 20 points.

Three of a Kind: Any 3 cards of the same rank score 30.5. (e.g., 3 Sevens = 30.5 points)

Losing Coins

After hands are revealed:

  • If one or more players have less than 20 points, every player below 20 loses 1 coin.
  • Otherwise, the player with the lowest score loses 1 coin.
  • If multiple players tie for the lowest score, each tied player loses 1 coin.
  • A player can lose at most 1 coin per round.

Examples

24, 18, 16, 22 → The players with 18 and 16 each lose 1 coin.

24, 22, 27, 25 → The player with 22 loses 1 coin.

Special Rules

First Turn: Your first action each round must be Trade One Card.

Matching Center: If all 3 center cards are the same suit, you must Trade Three Cards.

31 Rule: If you reach 31, immediately reveal your hand. All players reveal their hands and the round ends immediately.
