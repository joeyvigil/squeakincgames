Gorbino's Quest

  • Joey Vigil
  • Aug 23, 2023

Gorbino's Quest is a strategy game of economic development and civilization building in a fantasy setting for 2-6 players aged 13 and up.

Rules

Rules available here: Gorbino's Quest Rules

Inspired by Concordia (2013), Mosaic: A Story of Civilization (2022), and Brass: Lancashire (2007); Gorbino's Quest combines the elements of tag collecting, hand management, resource management, income, loans, market, investments, resource production and area control. GB is an action selecting game. On your turn,first gain income marked by the income marker, then choose between 7 actions. Actions include:

Loan

Trade income for ₡ coins

Production

Produce resources at the providence of your choice

Trade

Buy and sell up to two different resource types

Development

Purchase Technology cards that contain tags as well as upgraded actions

Reset

Place some of your discarded cards back into your hand, gain income, and cycle build decks

Build

Build at a certain province with province build cards. Build with a certain resource with resource build cards. Or just normal build by building next to one of your current cities.

Treachery

Unleash cunning schemes to hinder your opponents.

Choosing a wise sequence of actions will have the player gain their desired income, actions, resource production, cities and tags.

Game End

The end of the game is triggered after the last Technology card is purchased. Players receive Victory Points from Tags and Achievements. The player with the most Victory points is the winner. Tag VPs (victory points) are shown below:

Pink Tags: Consistency

VPs = 8 times number of pink tags

Purple Tags: Wealth

VPs = Number of 20 ₡ coins times number of purple tags

Yellow Tags: Polycentric

VPs = Number of cities times number of yellow tags

Blue Tags: Abundance

VPs = Number of resource production types times number of blue tags x2

Red Tags: Dominance

VPs = Number of provinces where you have the most cities times number of red tags x3

Green Tags: Metropolis

VPs = Number of provinces times number of green tags x2

Achievements

Worth 6 VP each and gained by being the first to accomplish certain objectives:

  • 4 Yellow Tags, 4 Green Tags, 4 Blue Tags, 4 Red Tags, 4 Pink Tags, 4 Purple Tags
  • 5 Cities
  • 4 Providences
  • 3 Providences where you have the most cities
  • 4 Production Types
  • 50 ₡ coins
  • Buy last Technology card and trigger end of game
