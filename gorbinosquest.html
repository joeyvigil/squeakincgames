Player Turn

Play a card and discard it, cards include:

  • Loan: Reduce income and gain money
  • Trade - Buy/sell up to two types of goods in any amount
  • Production -Produce resources
  • Development - Purchase technology cards and place them in your hand
  • Reset- Place cards from your discard into their hand
  • Treachery - Steal resources, buy resources for a low cost, or take the actions of other players
  • Build - build a city at an adjacent location

Each Round

After all player have taken their turn, do the following:

  1. Advance Technology Track: Discard the rightmost technology card from the track and shift all remaining cards to the right.
  2. Gain/Pay coins based on the income tracker.
  3. Rotate 1st player marker clockwise, that player starts their turn.

Scoring

Game ends when no cards remain on the Technology Track

  • Score calculated by how many 'tags' players have
  • And by using the scoring section of the board

Choosing a wise sequence of actions will have the player gain their desired income, actions, resource production, tags and ultimately victory points.
